Do higher user ratings equal higher earnings?

Hover over the dots to see income

Compares the data from Gross Earnings vs the Meta Score which shows if the User scores reflect the money the movie will make

  • Usually people assume that the good user scores from Meta scores means that the movie will make money but that doesn't appear to be true
  • We can see some of the higher scores lead to high earnings but we also see some lower scores with higher earnings
  • We also see a cluster of movies around the 70-90% meta score of movies that did well earnings wise so higher scores don't instantly mean more as those clusters are in the lower percentages

This doesn't prove that a high user rating instantly equals higher earnings
